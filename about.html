  • Recording Engineer (Bienen School of Music, 2019-2021)
  • Technology Manager (Bienen School of Music, 2019-2021)
  • Music Editor (Bienen School of Music, 2020-2021)
  • Piano Accompanist (Northwestern University, 2017-2019)

Though all of these work experiences are based in audio, they require different subsets of technological knowledge. Through music technology and computer science courses in programming and data structures, I found the skills required to undertake such a wide breadth in not only audio, but design and coding as well.
